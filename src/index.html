Experience

8 years in Fintech

While working in Germany and the U.K. I've helped teams design apps for payments, banking, investments and retail trading. During this time I’ve chatted with countless people about their finances and learnt so much along the way. This experience helps me spot more risks, possible improvements and potential opportunities throughout the design process.

Approach

Entire team engagement

As a trained facilitator, I engage the entire product team in the design process. Sharing user insights, encouraging a broader range of ideas and quick experimentation is not only more fun, it's key to developing a shared understanding of users' problems across the team. This ultimately results in more innovative, useful and higher quality software built faster.
