About Me

Scott’s been into tech since his Grandad shoved a Commodore Amiga keyboard and mouse into his chubby little hands aged three. Now, as a UX designer with over 7 years experience at Fintech companies in Germany and the UK, he’s helped product teams design award winning apps for payments, banking, investments and retail trading. When not designing, he’s thinking up novel ways to improve designer and dev collaboration, to improve user understanding and products… all while having a tonne of fun in the process.
